What is Serenity Sketch?

A Guided Path to Inner Quiet and Creativity. Serenity Sketch is a digital experience you can play on your phone, tablet, or PC. It's designed for those who seek the quiet relief of mindfulness, and are drawn to the process of creation.

Let Go with Water Painting

Discover the ancient practice of painting with water. There are no mistakes, no permanent marks. Just a simple, soothing canvas that evaporates as you create, focusing you purely on the present moment.

Guided Prompts, Not Goals

Gentle voice prompts and self-reflection questions guide you through your session. These are invitations to explore, not tasks to complete, helping you connect with your creativity and feel more grounded.

Explore Inviting Environments

Calming soundscapes usher you into the present moment, so your painting flows freely. As you explore, encounter new brushes and tools for interacting with the world. Each session transports you to a unique natural setting:

  • An ancient forest
  • A serene ocean bay
  • Mysterious cliffs along the shore
  • A lively wood
